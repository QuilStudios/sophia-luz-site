The Serenity Collection

The Serenity collection began as a private ritual. A way back to the body when the world got too loud.

Each painting is oil pastel on handmade cotton paper, worked slowly by hand until the texture tells you something words don't. Small in size. Particular in feeling. There is only one of each.
